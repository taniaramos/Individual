It has been suggested in, Introduction to Design, the creation of a vinyl package on the basis of a sound. The sound I chose was "Radioactive" by "Imagine Dragons", but only the instrumental. I started to listen the music and drawing patterns depending on the pace. Then i arranged the patterns according to the time and i scattered them by the faces of the cube, whitch i constructed with K-line. The colors i chose were dark colors with purpose to relate to the music. Inside the package I put a false bottom of a contrasting color to be the surprise factor. The vinyl slice also contains the same external standards. Finally we had to develop a poster advertising a concert by the band of the selected sound. I picked up once again the same patterns that I used throughout the entire project.
