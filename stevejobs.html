STEVE JOBS

Third Year of University

Final art

Application on mockup

The brief was to create a visual identity of a famous personality. The only requirement was the perception of the face. The personality I chose was Steve Jobs. I opted for a digital vector illustration, with strong lines and features, as I felt that was the way that would better reflect his personality. Lastly, the identity created was applied on a T-shirt.
